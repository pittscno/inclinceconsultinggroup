Executive Board



Hannah Reiling - President

Hannah is a junior at the University of Pittsburgh double majoring in Computer Science and Political Science. After college, she plans on either pursuing a career in software engineering or exploring technology policy. In addition to Incine Consulting Group, Hannah is a facilitator for Girls Who Code as well as an ambassador and peer mentor for the University Honors College. She also helped organize the 2018 She Innovates Hackathon as the Mentor Recruiter. Hannah loves being a part of Incline Consulting Group because it allows her to engage with Pittsburgh beyond the "Pitt bubble" and have a positive impact on the local community.

Michael Urzillo - Executive Vice President

Michael is a rising senior at the University of Pittsburgh studying Industrial Engineering and pursuing a certificate in Supply Chain Management. Michael is currently serving as the Executive Vice President to Incline Consulting Group, after previously serving as a project manager and then executive board intern. Michael also works as an Industrial Engineering Co-Op at Thermo Fisher Scientific in Pittsburgh, PA. The dual ability of ICG to provide non-profits in the area over 3,000 hours of volunteering each semester, combined with the opportunities of work and client experience that it offers the students makes Incline Michael’s favorite organization that he’s involved with on campus.

Brian Forristal - VP of Corporate Relations

Brian is a junior studying Statistics at the University of Pittsburgh. Prior to studying at Pitt, Brian was a Territory Supervisor for American Tower Corporation for nearly 4 years. After graduating high school, he served in the United States Marine Corps and deployed to places like Haiti, after the 2010 earthquake, to provide humanitarian assistance. Brian intends to become a consultant post-graduation where he hopes to effectively leverage his prior business experience and statistical-analysis skillset.
